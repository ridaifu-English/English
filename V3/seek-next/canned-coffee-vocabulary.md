## Part 1: Key Vocabulary Matching

**Match the words from the story with their meanings:**

| Word | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| incident | a) A large public exhibition or fair |
| accustomed | b) To increase very quickly |
| rapidly | c) A single event or happening |
| exposition | d) Familiar with something through experience |
| trial and error | e) A method of solving problems by trying different solutions until one works |

**Answers:**
1. incident - c) A single event or happening
2. accustomed - d) Familiar with something through experience
3. rapidly - b) To increase very quickly
4. exposition - a) A large public exhibition or fair
5. trial and error - e) A method of solving problems by trying different solutions until one works
