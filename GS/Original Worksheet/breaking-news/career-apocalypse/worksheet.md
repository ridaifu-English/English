## Career Apocalypse - Worksheet

### Part 1: Gap Fill Exercise

Listen to the article and fill in the blanks.

Many young people in the USA do not have jobs. They are not in school. This is a big problem. Over 4 million young people (1) _________ work or study. People call this a "career apocalypse." Many young people feel worried. They cannot find (2) _________ jobs. Some young people do not have the right skills. Companies want workers with experience. But young people (3) _________ experience. The UK has the same problem. Many young people there do not work or study. Experts say we need to help young people. We need to (4) _________ them new skills. Schools and companies should work together. Young people need more training. They need help to find jobs. Some people say the problem is very serious. They worry about the (5) _________. If young people do not work, the economy will suffer. Governments are trying to fix this problem. They want to create more (6) _________ for young people.
