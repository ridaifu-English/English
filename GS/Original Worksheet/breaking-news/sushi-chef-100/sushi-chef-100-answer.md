## Part 5: Critical Thinking

**Open-ended question - answers will vary**

Sample answer points students might include:
- Advantages: Staying active keeps the mind and body healthy, gives purpose and meaning, sharing expertise with others, feeling productive
- Disadvantages: Risk of overworking, missing time with family, potential health concerns, need for rest and relaxation
- Students should reference Jiro Ono's belief that "the best medicine is to work"
- Cultural differences in attitudes toward work and retirement
- Personal choice and finding what makes each individual happy
- Balance between passion for work and taking care of one's health
