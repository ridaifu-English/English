Monkey : I have good news. I heard Lion is very sick. He is going to die soon.  
Fox     : That's great!  
Deer    : Yes! When he's gone, we ( ① ) walk around the forest without being afraid of him.  
Monkey  : Lion said he was sorry for the way he treated* us. He decided to leave us a fortune*.  
Deer    : That's nice of him. ( ② )  
Monkey  : I have no idea. Let's go visit him and find out!

Lion's voice : The next one may come in.  
Deer        : ( ④ )May I go next?

Fox         : I'm getting worried. ( ⑤ )There's something that's not right.
