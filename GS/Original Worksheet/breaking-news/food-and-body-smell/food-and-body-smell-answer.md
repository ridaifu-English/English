## Part 5: Critical Thinking

**Open-ended question - answers will vary**

Sample answer points students might include:
- Advantages: Better social interactions, increased confidence, professional appearance
- Disadvantages: Missing out on healthy foods (broccoli, garlic have health benefits), restrictive diet may be unhealthy
- Students should reference the health benefits of foods like garlic (antibacterial properties), broccoli (vitamins and nutrients), and coffee (antioxidants)
- Balance between health and social concerns
- Cultural differences in what smells are considered acceptable
- Alternatives like personal hygiene instead of avoiding healthy foods
