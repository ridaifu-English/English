## Part 5: Critical Thinking

**Open-ended question - answers will vary**

Sample answer points students might include:
- Advantages: Astronauts could have fresh food, reduce dependence on Earth supplies, make long space missions more sustainable, provide psychological benefits of gardening
- Disadvantages: Expensive research, requires special equipment and soil, might not work for all plants or locations (like Mars), requires ongoing maintenance
- Students should reference the challenges of growing in space mentioned in the article (special soil needed, different results on Moon vs Mars)
- Balance between practical benefits for astronauts and the costs/difficulties of space agriculture
- Cultural importance of familiar foods (like tea) for astronauts' morale
- Long-term sustainability of space exploration and colonization
