**The Ant and the Grasshopper**

One sunny summer day, an Ant worked hard collecting food. A Grasshopper relaxed nearby.

Grasshopper : Ant! Stop working and sing with me! It's a lovely day.

Ant         : Hello. I must gather food for winter now.

Grasshopper : Winter? Don't worry! There's lots of food now. Let's have fun!

Ant         : But winter will be cold with no food. We must prepare *now*.
            It is smart to get ready when it's ( ① ) ______.

Grasshopper : You worry too much! Winter is far away. I will sing today!
            ( ③ ) Nah, I'll think about it later! Bye!

(The Ant kept working. ( ② ) It carried food back and forth.)

| A | Winter came with cold snow. The hungry Grasshopper had no food. It went to the Ant's nest. |

Grasshopper : (Knocking weakly) Ant? Please, help me! I am so cold and hungry!
            Can you share some food?

Ant         : My food? What did you do all summer while I worked hard?

Grasshopper : I... I sang songs and enjoyed the sun. I didn't prepare. I see now
            it was a mistake.

| B | The Ant thought carefully about what the Grasshopper had said. |

Ant         : You sang all summer? Then perhaps you can dance all winter.
            I worked hard for this food. It is important to prepare.

| C | The Ant closed the door. The Grasshopper learned a hard lesson in the cold. |
