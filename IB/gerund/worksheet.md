## Part 2: Meaning Changes (15 points)

Choose the correct form based on the meaning. Write **G** (gerund) or **I** (infinitive).

1. I remember **______** (meet) her at the conference last year.  
   _Meaning: I recall that I met her in the past._

2. Remember **______** (lock) the door when you leave.  
   _Meaning: Don't forget to lock it in the future._

3. He tried **______** (open) the window, but it was stuck.  
   _Meaning: He made an effort to open it._

4. He tried **______** (use) a different key, and it worked!  
   _Meaning: He experimented with a different key._

5. I stopped **______** (rest) for a few minutes.  
   _Meaning: I paused in order to rest._
