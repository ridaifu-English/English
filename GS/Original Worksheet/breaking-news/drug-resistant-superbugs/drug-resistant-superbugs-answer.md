## Part 4: True or False

1. **T** (The article says the WHO warned about superbugs that drugs cannot kill)
2. **F** (One in six bugs were drug-resistant, not one in three; though in Southeast Asia it was 1 in 3)
3. **F** (Drug resistance is highest in Southeast Asia, not Africa)
4. **F** (The article says bugs are becoming drug-resistant faster than we can make new drugs)

## Part 5: Critical Thinking

**Open-ended question - answers will vary**

Sample answer points students might include:
- More testing and research to create new drugs
- Better use of existing drugs and antibiotics
- People should not use antibiotics unnecessarily
- Doctors should only prescribe drugs when necessary
- Hospitals need better hygiene practices to prevent spread
- Students should reference the challenges mentioned in the article (drug resistance increasing faster than new drugs are created)
