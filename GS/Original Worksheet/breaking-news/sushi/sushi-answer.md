## Part 5: Critical Thinking

**Open-ended question - answers will vary**

Sample answer points students might include:
- Advantages: Staying active keeps the mind and body healthy, provides purpose and meaning, shares skills and experience with others, maintains social connections
- Disadvantages: Physical strain on aging body, less time for rest and relaxation, may prevent younger people from advancing in their careers
- Students should reference Jiro Ono's example and his dedication to reaching perfection
- Consider cultural differences in attitudes toward work and retirement
