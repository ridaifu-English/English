## Sushi Chef Working at 100 - Worksheet

### Part 1: Gap Fill Exercise

Listen to the article and fill in the blanks.

A sushi chef in Japan is still working, on his (1) _________. His name is Jiro Ono. Earlier this week, Tokyo's governor asked him about his (2) _________ a long life. He replied, 'To work.' He said: 'Even at 100 years old, I'll try to work…The (3) _________ is to work.' He said he plans to keep working for five more years.

Jiro Ono was (4) _________ 1925. He started working when he was seven. He became a (5) _________ in 1951. He opened a small sushi bar in Tokyo. There, he spent many years trying to make the (6) _________. In 2011, a movie was made about Ono called 'Jiro Dreams of Sushi'. In the film, he talked about reaching 'perfection'.
