Plastic Bags vs. Reusable Bags: It is undoubtedly better for people to bring reusable bags from home rather than using plastic bags from stores. Plastic bags contribute significantly to pollution, clogging landfills and harming wildlife, especially marine animals. By using our own bags, we can drastically reduce single-use plastic consumption. This simple habit is a powerful collective action towards environmental conservation and a more sustainable future for everyone.
