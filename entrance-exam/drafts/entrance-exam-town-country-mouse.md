**The Town Mouse and the Country Mouse**

Once upon a time, a Town Mouse went to visit his cousin who lived in the countryside. The Country Mouse lived in a simple hole under a big tree.

Country Mouse : Welcome to my home, cousin! ( ① ) Please make yourself comfortable.
Town Mouse    : Thank you, but this place is so... simple. Don't you get bored here?
Country Mouse : Not at all! I have everything I need. Let me ( ② ) you some dinner.

| A | The Country Mouse brought out his simple food: some bread crumbs, a few grains, and water. |

Town Mouse    : Is this all you have to eat? This food is so plain and tasteless!
Country Mouse : ( あ ) It may be simple, but it's safe and healthy. I'm happy with what I have.
Town Mouse    : My dear cousin, you don't know what real food is! Come with me to the city.
                I'll show you how to live like a king!

| B | The next day, both mice traveled to the big city where the Town Mouse lived in a grand house. |

Town Mouse    : Look at this feast! Cheese, cake, meat, and wine! This is how I eat every day.
Country Mouse : Wow! I've never seen such delicious food before. ( い ) You're so lucky!
Town Mouse    : ( う ) Of course I am! City life is much better than country life.

Just as they began to eat, they heard loud footsteps approaching.

Town Mouse    : Oh no! The humans are coming! Quick, hide!

| C | Both mice ran as fast as they could and hid in a dark corner, their hearts beating fast with fear. |

After the humans left, they came out, but then a big cat appeared suddenly.

Cat          : Meow! I smell mice!
Town Mouse   : Run! The cat will catch us!

They escaped, but the Country Mouse was shaking with fear.

Country Mouse : I can't live like this! I'd rather have simple food in safety than fancy food with danger.
                Thank you, cousin, but I'm going home. ( ③ ) A simple life with peace
                is better than a rich life with fear.
