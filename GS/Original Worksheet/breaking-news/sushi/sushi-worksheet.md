## Sushi Master at 100 - Worksheet

#### Part 1: Gap Fill Exercise

Listen to the article and fill in the blanks.

A famous sushi chef in Japan is still working, on his 100th birthday. His name is Jiro Ono. At a ceremony, Tokyo's governor asked him about his secret to a long life. He replied, "(1) _________________." He said: "Even if I live to be 110 years old, I'll try to work...The important thing is to work." He said he plans to continue for another (2) _________________.

Jiro Ono was born in 1925. He started working when he was (3) _________________. He became a sushi chef in 1951. He opened a small (4) _________________ in Tokyo. There, he spent his career trying to make perfect sushi. In 2011, a (5) _________________ was made about Ono called "Jiro Dreams of Sushi". In the film, he talked about reaching (6) _________________.
