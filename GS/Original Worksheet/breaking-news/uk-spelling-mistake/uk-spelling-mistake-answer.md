## Part 5: Critical Thinking

**Open-ended question - answers will vary**

Sample answer points students might include:
- Spelling mistakes by political parties can damage their credibility and make them look unprofessional
- Small mistakes like this can happen to anyone and may not reflect on their actual policies
- It shows whether a party pays attention to detail and quality control
- Social media can amplify small mistakes and make them seem more important than they are
- Students should consider whether spelling errors affect a party's ability to govern effectively
