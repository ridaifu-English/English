# Space Development - Worksheet

## Part 1: Gap Fill Exercise

Listen to the dialogue and fill in the blanks with the correct connectors.

**John:** We are graduating from high school soon. What is your future goal, Mei?

**Mei:** Well, I'm interested in space, (1) __________ I want to be an astronaut if I have a chance.

**John:** Wow, that's cool. Why do you want to be an astronaut?

**Mei:** (2) __________ I was a child, I have loved watching stars. That has made me dream of seeing the Earth from space someday.

**John:** Oh. I heard many astronauts saying it was beautiful. What do you want to do as an astronaut?

**Mei:** I want to solve the problem of space debris, (3) __________ is waste flying around the Earth.

**John:** Space debris seems like a big problem these days, (4) __________ human beings have to do something.

**Mei:** Right. (5) __________ I want to join a mission to collect space debris. By doing so, people on the Earth can live safely and enjoy watching the beautiful stars.
