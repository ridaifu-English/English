### Nap Time at School - Worksheet

#### Part 1: Gap Fill Exercise

Listen to the dialogue and fill in the blanks with the correct connectors.

**Aya:** What's wrong with you, Ben? You look sleepy.

**Ben:** Yes, I am. I couldn't sleep well last night (1) __________ I took a long nap after lunch.

**Aya:** That often happened to me before, (2) __________ I found effective ways of taking a nap recently.

**Ben:** What are they?

**Aya:** (3) __________, you shouldn't sleep for too long. It's best to take a 10 to 20-minute nap.

**Ben:** I see. I slept for about 3 hours yesterday. That was too long. Anything else?

**Aya:** (4) __________, don't make the room dark and don't get into bed. (5) __________ you do so, you may fall into a deep sleep because your brain will think it is night-time.

---

#### Part 2: Listening Comprehension

Answer the following questions based on the dialogue.

1. What is Ben's problem?

2. How long should a good nap be according to Aya?

3. How long did Ben sleep yesterday?

4. What are the TWO things Aya says you should NOT do when taking a nap?

5. Why should you avoid making the room dark when napping?

#### Part 3: True or False

1. Ben looks energetic today. **T / F**
